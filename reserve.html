Anton's

We accept reservations seven days a week, up to 30 days in advance, via Resy.

For parties of nine or more, please email feasts@antonsnyc.com.

Please note, reservations are not available for bar seats. Anton’s is a neighborhood restaurant, and we always reserve a number of seats for walk-in guests in our dining room and at our bar. When the restaurant is full, we’re happy to take a name and number and let you know when a table becomes available.
